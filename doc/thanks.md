## Thanks

### Strudel & TidalCycles

[Strudel](https://strudel.cc) by Felix Roos brings the ideas of [TidalCycles](https://tidalcycles.org) by Alex McLean to the browser. The pattern language, the web component, and the whole approach of making live-coding accessible without installation is what makes this playground possible. Thank you for building such a joyful tool.

- [Strudel documentation](https://strudel.cc/learn)
- [Strudel Getting Started](https://strudel.cc/workshop/getting-started/)
- [TidalCycles](https://tidalcycles.org)

### CodeMirror

[CodeMirror](https://codemirror.net) by Marijn Haverbeke provides the editor. The modular architecture of CodeMirror 6 makes it easy to load exactly the features needed from a CDN, with no build step.

### The live-coding community

The broader live-coding and algorave community continues to push the boundaries of music and code as performance art. Thank you for the inspiration and the shared spirit of creative experimentation.

- [TOPLAP](https://toplap.org) — the home of live coding
- [Algorave](https://algorave.com) — algorithmic dance culture
